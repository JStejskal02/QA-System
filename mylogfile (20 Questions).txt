1Ques) Who was George Washington?
1A) George Washington (February 22, 1732 – December 14, 1799) was an American military officer, statesman, and Founding Father who served as the first president of the United States from 1789 to 1797.

2Ques) Who was Franklin D Roosevelt?
2A) Franklin Delano Roosevelt (January 30, 1882 – April 12, 1945), commonly known by his initials FDR, was an American politician who served as the 32nd president of the United States from 1933 until his death in 1945.

20Ques) Where is the Bermuda Triangle?
